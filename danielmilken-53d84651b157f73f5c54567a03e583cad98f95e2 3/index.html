What is "Level Up"


Inspired by the anime, Solo Leveling, by Chu-Gong, Level Up is an app with extensive workout plans,
which encourages players to exercise and practice healthy habits while immersing them into a video-game like environment.


How it Works

When you open up the app, you will be prompted to put in your information (age, gender, height, weight, exercise frequency, etc.)
After calculations, players can make a username for themselves and will gain access to the app.

Leveling up is simple, but hard work. The player must complete daily quests to gain XP. Gaining XP raises your level, rank, and the difficulties of the workouts. Ultimately, the player will become stronger and rise the ranks, becoming the best.
